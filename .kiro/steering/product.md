# Trellis — Wiki-Method Lab

The upstream repository for the Trellis wiki method: the normative schema, scripts, and instantiation seed, plus a dogfood research wiki whose single goal is to develop and evidence the wiki method itself, through its own operation.

## Purpose

- Ingest academic papers, articles, conversations, and other source material
- Produce structured, interlinked wiki pages with provenance tracking
- Maintain a living synthesis that evolves as new sources are added
- Support queries against accumulated knowledge with cited answers

## Users

- The workspace owner (researcher) who drops sources and asks questions
- The research agent that processes sources and maintains the wiki
- Potential subagents delegated specific tasks (search, synthesis, formatting)
