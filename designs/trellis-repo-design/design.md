- **Repo-shape rationale** (the name, the two-surface layout, seeded AGENTS.md, path-preserving plain-copy migration) — [[wiki/llm-wiki/decisions/trellis-repo-bootstrap]].
- **Split rationale and federation alternatives** — [[wiki/llm-wiki/decisions/adopt-single-goal-federation]].
- **Instantiation and upgrades** — `seed/interview.md` (the questionnaire → manifest flow and the canonical upgrade prompt).
- **The still-live federation design** (principles, topology, inheritance breadcrumbs, log taxonomy) — [[designs/wiki-federation-and-inheritance]].
