# Andrej Karpathy

## Identity

Creator of the [[wiki/llm-wiki/constructs/llm-wiki-pattern]]. Former director of AI at Tesla, founding member of OpenAI. AI educator and researcher.

## Relevance to the Wiki

In April 2026, Karpathy posted on X about using LLMs to build personal knowledge bases rather than primarily for code generation. The post went viral (16M+ views) and the follow-up GitHub Gist hit 5,000+ stars within days. His wiki on a single research topic reached ~100 articles and ~400,000 words.

## Notes

### Intellectual Evolution
1. **Vibe Coding** (Feb 2025) — Accept AI-generated code without line-by-line review. Trust the model, test the output.
2. **Agentic Engineering** (Jan 2026) — Humans orchestrate AI agents rather than writing code directly.
3. **LLM Knowledge Bases** (Apr 2026) — AI manages knowledge, not just code. The human is a curator, not a writer.

Each phase shifts more cognitive labor to the LLM while keeping humans in the loop for judgment and direction.

### Key Quote
> "The LLM writes and maintains all of the data of the wiki. I rarely touch it directly."
